# Content Patterns

Knowledge videos must teach one idea clearly. Prefer mental models, examples, and visual structure over generic inspirational copy.

## Script Rules

- Start with a concrete tension, not "today we will talk about".
- Explain terms before using acronyms heavily.
- Add an example within the first half of PM methodology videos.
- Add limitations for AI concept videos.
- Avoid fake precision. If a number is illustrative, say so.
- Keep narration around 250-280 Chinese characters per minute or 140-160 English words per minute.

## Scene Density

| Scene Duration | Max Primary Ideas | Recommended Visual |
|---|---:|---|
| under 3s | 1 phrase | title card or contrast word |
| 3-6s | 1 idea plus 1 support | card, step, simple diagram |
| 6-10s | 1 idea plus example | flow, mini dashboard, comparison |
| over 10s | split unless narration is slow | chapter section |

## Good Hooks

- "MVP 不是低配版产品。"
- "RAG 不是万能知识库。"
- "PRD 最重要的不是功能列表。"
- "Agent 不是会聊天的工作流。"
- "北极星指标不是老板最关心的数字。"

## Avoid

- Long paragraph subtitles.
- Stock footage that does not explain the idea.
- Repeating the same sentence in title, body text, and captions.
- Using AI-generated claims without a source.
- Mixing too many frameworks in one short video.
